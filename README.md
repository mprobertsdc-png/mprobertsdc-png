# 👤 About Me

Hi there! I'm **Dr. Megan Paige Roberts**. I hold a Doctorate of Chiropractic degree and actively practice in Kentucky. I am deeply dedicated to clinical excellence, authentic patient relationships, and high-quality healthcare service.

---

## 💬 How I Communicate
* **Primary Channel:** **Texting** is my preferred method of contact.
* **Response Times:** I am generally very prompt, especially with important matters. 
* **Boundaries:** Please note that my response time varies based on patient volume and burnout. I do not respond to texts after **8:00 PM** until the following business day.

## 🎨 My Tone & Style
* **In Person & Discussion:** My tone is analytical yet casual, authoritative yet warm. I naturally tend to speak fairly quickly.
* **Feedback Preference:** Please be direct and specific. I appreciate it when people say: *"Hey, I'm going to give you feedback—take it or leave it, it's not personal."* Avoid vague generalizations and provide concrete examples instead.

## 🌟 Core Values
* **Autonomy & Efficiency:** I value independent clinical judgment and streamlined, effective processes.
* **Authenticity & Excellence:** I show up genuinely and hold myself and my work to the highest clinical standards.
* **Service:** My practice is rooted in helping others and delivering exceptional care to my community.

## 🛠️ My Preferences & Working Style
* **Meetings:** I heavily prefer meetings that have a clear, pre-defined **agenda**.
* **Decision Making:** I do not rush major choices. I make my best decisions after I have prayed about them and allowed the Holy Spirit to guide me.
